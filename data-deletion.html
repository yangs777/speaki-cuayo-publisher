Data Deletion Instructions

Creators who have connected a social media account to Speaki Cuayo Publisher may request deletion of account data associated with the service, including data connected through YouTube, TikTok, Meta, Instagram, X, or other supported platform APIs.

Send an email to yangs77771@gmail.com with the subject line "Speaki Cuayo Publisher Data Deletion" and include the connected platform name and account username or profile identifier. Do not include passwords.

After receiving a valid request, we will delete or de-identify locally stored access tokens, account identifiers, publishing records, and support records associated with the requesting account where applicable. For YouTube, this may include locally stored YouTube OAuth tokens, channel or video metadata caches, comment triage records, and reply draft logs. Some records may be retained if required for security, legal compliance, or platform policy reasons.

You can also revoke platform access directly in the relevant platform's account settings. Revoking access prevents future use of that platform connection by the service.

To revoke Google or YouTube access, visit your Google Account permissions page and remove access for the connected app.
