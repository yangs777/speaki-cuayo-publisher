Speaki Cuayo Publisher

A web-based publishing assistant and owner-operated YouTube API toolset for creators who prepare original short-form videos, maintain channel metadata, manage localizations, and review creator replies.

Speaki Cuayo Publisher helps approved creators organize short videos, review captions and metadata, and send selected content to connected platforms after the creator authorizes each connection through the platform's official login flow. For YouTube, the owner-operated workflow uses the official YouTube Data API to maintain the Speaki Cuayo channel, update metadata and localizations, and manage approved creator replies.

Creator-controlled publishing Videos are selected by the creator before they are uploaded or prepared as a draft for review.
Connected account authorization The service uses OAuth permissions requested only for account identification and content publishing.
YouTube API maintenance YouTube access is used for owner-authorized channel metadata, localizations, comment triage, and approved creator replies.
Short-form workflow Captions, hashtags, posting options, and publishing records are kept focused on creator video workflows.
